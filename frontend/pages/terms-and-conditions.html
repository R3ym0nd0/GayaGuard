Account and Service Guidelines

Terms and Conditions

Please read these basic terms before using the Barangay Gaya Gaya Request Screening and Monitoring System.

Use of the System

This system is intended to help residents submit and monitor barangay requests using a more organized digital process.

Accurate Information

Users must provide truthful, complete, and updated information when creating an account or submitting requests.

Account Responsibility

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Activity performed through your account may be treated as your own submission.

Screening and Review

Requests may go through screening, scoring, and review by authorized barangay staff before any final decision is recorded.

Supporting Documents

Uploaded files should be relevant and appropriate for the selected request. Incomplete or misleading documents may delay processing.

Privacy Notice

Account details and submitted request information may be stored and viewed by authorized administrators for processing, monitoring, and records management.

System Changes

The barangay may update procedures, features, or review steps whenever needed to improve the service and request handling workflow.
