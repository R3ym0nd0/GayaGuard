About the System

Building better public services through digital transformation

Official Barangay Seal
Barangay Gaya Gaya

The Barangay Gaya Gaya Request Screening and Monitoring System is a dedicated digital platform created to modernize barangay administrative operations.

This system enables residents to submit requests online while providing barangay staff with tools to efficiently screen, review, track, and manage all incoming requests in one centralized location.

With organized records, transparent tracking, and smart screening support, we aim to improve service delivery, reduce processing time, and build trust through accountability and efficiency.

Barangay Services Covered

All official barangay requests available through our digital portal

How It Works

Simple and transparent request processing workflow

1

Submit Request

Resident submits request online with complete requirements and information

2

System Screening

System validates requirements and performs initial screening checks automatically

3

Staff Review

Barangay staff reviews request, verifies information, and makes decision

4

Decision & Monitoring

Request is processed, resident is notified, and status is tracked until completion
